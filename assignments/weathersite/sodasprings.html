This Day in History

Not only is it Independence Day, Soda Springs is also celebrating 100 years of Caribou County! "Soda Springs was established as the county seat of Oneida County when it was created in January 1864. Soda Springs is named after and famous for its hundreds of natural springs of carbonated water. The Soda Springs Geyser is the only captive geyser in the world. It is let loose every hour on the hour by a timed release valve. Its height and volume have not decreased after all these years."
