Services and Amenities

The Temple Inn & Suites is a hotel chain headquartered in Bethesda, MD that builds and manages specialized hotel accomodations near temple locations of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints around the world. The owner-operated company caters to temple workers, patrons, and their families by providing specialized services and accomodations that meet the needs of those patrons. The hotel accomodations and services are available to anyone willing to abide by the terms and policies outlined by the company.

Specialized Services

  • Free access to changing rooms and bathroom facilities
  • Playroom and low-cost, short-term babysitting for children of patrons
  • Long-term stay accommodations in kitchenette suites for full-time temple missionaries
  • Temple history themed reception and sitting area
  • Customizable wedding reception hall with a simple catering menu and kitchen access
  • Family search center that supports family search and ordinance work

Free Amenities

  • Breakfast
  • Workout room
  • Indoor pool
  • Wifi high-speed internet access and smart TVs
  • Parking
  • Temple Shuttle
  • Family history consultation and family ordinance card service
