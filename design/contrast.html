Contrast

Company Name

Spartan Design University

How Used

Contrast refers to the arrangement of opposite elements: light vs. dark colors, rough vs. smooth textures, large vs. small shapes, etc.; in a piece so as to create visual interest, excitement and drama. This image portrays contrasting colors and shapes (circles and squares).
